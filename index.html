About Us

Canada's Trusted Experts in
Metal Siding, Cladding & Roofing
Solutions

Since 2016, The Edge Sheet Metal & Aluminum Ltd. has been delivering premium exterior systems across Canada. With over 9 years of hands-on expertise, we specialize in metal siding, cladding, fascia and gutter systems, roofing solutions, and custom panel fabrication. From sourcing top-grade materials to precision installations, our end-to-end services are built to match the vision and demands of each unique project.

  • Custom aluminum solutions for diverse industries
  • Trusted partner for industrial-grade projects
